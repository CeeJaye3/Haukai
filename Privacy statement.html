Haukai Restaurant Privacy Statement

When you fill in our contact or reservations form we will require the following personal information from you:

    Name.

    Contact information.

    Message if applicable.

We collect this information from you in order to complete your reservation and be able to get in touch with you if neccessary.

All information is required in order for us to complete your reservation or enquiry. This is because if you did not provide contact details we would be unable to reply to your enquiry or contact you regarding your reservation.

Your information will not be held on file after your enquiry is dealt with or your reservation is complete.

A copy of your reservation can be sent to you by selecting the applicable option on the form.

You can contact us for a copy of your information at admin@HaukaiRestaurant.co.nz
